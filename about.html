A Few Words About Me

I am a Business Administration student at the University of Northern Colorado. I absolutely love Houseplants and have been cultivating my knowledge on them for over 5 years now. Once I recieve my business degree, I plan on opening my own retail plant boutique to bring my knowledge of business and my passion for plants together!

Educational Background

I am a dedicated student currently working on the attainment of my Bachelors of Science in General Business Administration. Before coming to UNC, I attended Front Range Community College in Longmont, Colorado and recevied my Associates of Arts degree in General Studies. I wasn't completely sure what I wanted to do just yet, but I knew I loved plants and psychology so most of my studies at FRCC consisted of horticultural and psychology classes. I eventually decided that my goal was to open my own plant boutique, and thus I chose to pursue a business degree to help me gain entreprenuerial knowledge to assist in running a small business.

Professional Background

I have worked for a variety of different companies and have continuously elected to work for smaller, family owned businesses. My love for plants stems from my very first job at a local nursery and garden center. Currently, I am amployed at a local flower farm in Berthoud, Colorado, as well as working as a Cloud Platform Engineer for a national telecommunications company. I strive to gain knowledge in every aspect of my life and actively look to gain new skills. Having a wide background in many different fields has allowed me to be open to any opportunity that comes my way!

More About Me

When I'm not attending to my responsibilities or caring for my extensive plant collection, I spend my time exploring Colorado and traveling with my lovely boyfriend, my best friends, or my family. I absolutely love the outdoors and enjoy water sports such as Kayaking and Paddleboarding! I also have many different pets that I love with my whole heart. I have two Saint Bernards, Mara and Indie, a Shorkie named Beau, Ozzy (the coolest cat around), and Alfred the Goldfish.
